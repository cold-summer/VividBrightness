# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

VividBrightness contributors and maintainers are expected to keep project spaces respectful, technically constructive, and welcoming.

Examples of positive behavior include:

- Giving specific, evidence-based feedback
- Respecting differing experience levels and viewpoints
- Accepting responsibility and correcting mistakes
- Prioritizing the health of the project and its community

Harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, threats, deliberate disruption, and publication of private information are not acceptable.

## Scope

This policy applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, reviews, and other public spaces where a person represents the project.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove inappropriate contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation. Report conduct concerns privately to a maintainer using the contact information on their GitHub profile. Do not disclose another person's private information in a public issue.
